Founders

Sara Reid

Chief Executive Officer

Sara Reid is a digital marketing professional responsible for the creation and founding of The Playground, an online platform for children's puzzle games. Before creating The Playground, Sara was Chief Of Marketing at Yavli, a rising London startup specialising in marketing and sponsored content, for young and millenial audiences. After founding The Playground, Sara took on the role of managing projects however has recently refocused herself on business oversite and content strategy.

Bob Sawyer

Chief Operating Officer

Bob Sawyer is a Cambridge Computer Science graduate who co-founded The Playground. Bob sucessfully juggled University and managing his own startup, however has recently moved on to persuing the role of COO full-time with The Playground. Bob passionately believes in inspiring future generations through the power of technology and views The Playgorund the platform through which he and others can make this dream a reality. As COO, Bob is responsible for The Playground's day to day operations and ensures that projects and new content are always delivered on schedule.

Aaron Swift

Chief Financial Officer

Aaron Swift made his name at Seedrs, an online equity crowdfunding platform. Whilst at the company, Aaron liased with clients to ensure that they had the best possible chance at sucessfully funding their business. After helping a number of startups develop and flourish into fully sustainable businesses, Aaron has most recently set his sights on a more permanent role, helping The Playground to mature and expand its porfolio of digital products. As Chief Financial Officer, Aaron manages The Playground's finances and advises on the planning and development of new products using his incredibly diverse industry experience.
